Mechanism of Action

How JXL-069 Works

JXL-069 operates through a metabolic switch mechanism distinct from any existing follicle compound in the research literature.

⚡
MPC Inhibition
JXL-069 selectively inhibits the mitochondrial pyruvate carrier (MPC1/2 complex), blocking pyruvate entry into the TCA cycle in follicle stem cells and forcing a shift in energy substrate utilisation.
Primary Target
🔬
Metabolic Reprogramming
Blocking oxidative phosphorylation shifts stem cell metabolism toward glycolysis — a metabolic state strongly associated with active proliferation and cell cycle re-entry in quiescent hair follicle stem cells.
Warburg-Like Switch
🧬
Stem Cell Activation
Dormant bulge-region stem cells are reactivated within 24-48 hours of exposure, measurable by Ki67 proliferation marker upregulation. This initiates the anagen (growth) phase of the follicular cycle.
Ki67 Confirmed

Research Protocol

Usage Guidelines

Standard laboratory application protocol for JXL-069 topical serum.

Step 01
Prepare Application Site
Ensure the target area is clean and dry. Remove any product residue prior to application to optimise compound absorption.
Step 02
Apply 1-2 Sprays
Dispense 1-2 sprays of JXL-069 directly to the scalp surface. Each actuation delivers approximately 0.25mL of the nanoliquid formulation.
Step 03
Allow Absorption
Allow the formulation to absorb fully. Do not rinse. The nanoliquid carrier is designed for rapid follicular penetration without residue.
Step 04
Repeat Daily
Standard research protocol: once daily application. Morning application recommended for consistency with research observation windows.
⚗️
Recommended Research Dosing 1-2 sprays to scalp daily (≈ 0.25-0.5mL per application). At 30mL per bottle, this equates to 60-120 daily applications per unit. Phase 2a protocol used once-daily application over an 8-week observation period.
